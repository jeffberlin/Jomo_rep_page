Who are we looking for? Active UNCC Students.

This doesn’t necessarily mean you have to work out all day. You could enjoy studying with others, having beers with friends, video games, yoga, swimming, fraternity parties, sports… you get the point. We just want you to do what you love with others on campus who enjoy the same.

You can work participate as much, or as little as you would like. The more you post, the more you make.

Compensation

So, you are paying me to have fun doing what I’m already doing. Why?

It’s simple, we want to connect UNCC students to everything going on around them and, odds are, you’re not the only person on campus that loves the things you do. So, we need your help to make that happen.
