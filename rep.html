Interested in becoming a JOMO Campus Rep and getting paid to have fun?

What is JOMO?

Who’s a typical JOMO Campus Rep?

Anyone who likes doing things with other people—so just about everyone! It could be big events like a concert, or small everyday things like studying with others, having beers with friends, playing video games, yoga, swimming, fraternity parties, sports… you get the point.

What does a JOMO Rep do?

Basically, all you need to do is what you’re already doing—things like studying, working out, grabbing dinner, doing laundry, playing video games, etc. And then when you want others to join you, let them know by posting on JOMO. And you can “work” as much, or as little as you like. It’s up to you, but the more you post, the more you make.

Here’s how it works…

We'll pay you $1 for every time you post

And then we'll pay you $1 for every person who joins you - so share with friends!

How do I sign up and get started?

  • Apply Below
  • Download JOMO
  • Share JOMO with others
  • Post some of the things you do everyday
  • Get paid!

So, you are paying me to have fun doing what I’m already doing. Why?

So, it’s simple. We want to connect UNCC students to everything going on around them and, odds are, you’re not the only person on campus that loves doing the things you do. So, we need your help to make that happen.

If you have any questions, you can email rep@jomoapp.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
